Ruth Script - Chain Stitch Embroidery (Font)
A chain stitch embroidery font, inspired by Ruth Franklin's hand lettered 'Artistic Script'

“Ruth Script” is a beautiful hand-lettered script font created for chain stitch embroidery and vintage style designs. The font is inspired by the “Artistic Script” alphabet designed by Ruth Franklin in the mid-century. I used her original alphabet as reference and added extended characters and numbers.

The result is a revival font that has the quirks and feel of a hand-lettered alphabet but is translated for use with your digital designs.

This font set includes two styles: outline and filled. It includes upper and lowercase letters, number characters, punctuation and some common symbols in the OTF format.

If you prefer to layout your designs by hand without the help of a font, you can reference the scanned version in The Lettering Book on page 94. I’ve also designed a digital poster that can be printed at scale or imported into procreate/photoshop/illustrator.

You can download it for free here.

Purchasing directly supports the cost of hosting this website as well as the many hours that went into making the font available from Ruth’s original material.
