Making Embroidered Emblems
Chenille embroidery and tutorials for creating award letters, mascots and satin stitch emblems.

The third book in Ruth’s embroidery business publications is 74 pages long and teaches how to expertly apply chenille/moss embroidery.

The book is full of award letters, mascots and satin stitch examples and Ruth gives a small tutorial on how to recreate each and every sample. She even gives a tutorial on how she embroidered the Indian chief found on Ray Price’s custom suit that Ruth embroidered when she worked for Nudie Cohn.

Embroidered Emblem’s topics also include swiss type satin-stitch and the introduction of screen printing in the embroidery process.
